Dual-Role System
Be Both Rider AND Pilot

Same app, same account, two modes. Toggle between "I need a ride" and "I'm driving, can help" anytime. Your commute can actually pay for itself.

Real Example:
Morning: Raj pays ₹70 (rider) →

Flexible Pricing
Pilots Can Waive Fares

Platform suggests a fair price, but pilot has final say. They can accept, reduce, or waive entirely. "You're my neighbor's son — no charge."

This enables:
• College seniors helping juniors free
• Neighbors reducing fares
• Emergency rides at zero cost
• Humanity > Algorithm
